## ü™Ñ The Magic (How it Works)
We don't just "read" the rules; we understand them. Our backend operates like a digital legal team working 24/7 for you.

### 1. The Analyst (GreenPT)
Our custom **GreenPT** model reads government websites like a human expert would. It doesn't just copy-paste text; it extracts the *logic* behind the benefits. It converts complex sentences like *"You must earn less than ‚Ç¨30.000, unless you have 3 children"* into precise, executable code.

### 2. The Auditor (Gemini 2.0 Flash)
We know AI can make mistakes. That's why we have a second layer. **Gemini 2.0 Flash** acts as the auditor, reviewing the logic generated by GreenPT. It runs test cases and "challenges" the system to prove its own accuracy before a user ever sees the data. **Zero hallucinations allowed.**

### 3. The Matchmaker (Neuro-Symbolic Engine)
When you search for help, we don't just keyword match. We run your profile against thousands of verified logic rules instantly. 
- **Strict Logic**: Tells you exactly what you qualify for.
- **Fuzzy Logic**: Tells you when you're *almost* there (e.g., you earn ‚Ç¨50 too much for a strict cutoff), so you don't miss out on opportunities because of a technicality.
